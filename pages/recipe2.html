Balsamic Reduction

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Utensils:

  • Medium saucepan

Directions:

Combine ingredients in a pan and bring to a boil.

Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.

Pour reduction into a jar and refridgerate for up to 1 month.
